# Persona: Student

## Name
Aisha Khan

## Role and context
Undergraduate student revising from lecture recordings and workshop materials using a local-first tool on a personal laptop.

## Goals
- Capture audio and slides per session.
- Generate clear notes and summaries quickly.
- Find key moments in a transcript during revision.

## Pain points
- Long recordings are hard to skim.
- Important details get lost across weeks.
- Tools that require accounts or cloud uploads feel risky.

## Key tasks
- Create modules and sessions.
- Upload audio and attachments.
- Generate AI notes and ask focused questions.
- Export a session pack for study.

## Success criteria
- Transcript is accurate enough to search.
- Notes are concise and align with the lecture.
- Exported files are easy to use offline.

## Constraints and environment
- Works offline or with limited connectivity.
- Runs on a mid-range laptop without GPU.
- Avoids sharing data with third-party services.
