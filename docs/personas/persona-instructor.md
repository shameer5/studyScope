# Persona: Instructor

## Name
Dr. Marcus Lee

## Role and context
Module lead who records workshops and wants to provide structured notes and references for students without relying on cloud services.

## Goals
- Organize sessions by module and week.
- Provide accurate, shareable summaries.
- Keep a clear record of what was taught.

## Pain points
- Manually writing summaries is slow.
- Students miss context if notes are incomplete.
- Tools with complex setup reduce adoption.

## Key tasks
- Create modules and sessions.
- Upload recordings and supporting materials.
- Review AI notes and adjust key points.
- Export session packs for distribution.

## Success criteria
- Notes match the session structure and emphasis.
- Students can find evidence in transcripts.
- Exports are consistent and reusable.

## Constraints and environment
- Limited time between sessions.
- Prefers local storage for IP-sensitive content.
- Needs a simple, repeatable workflow.
