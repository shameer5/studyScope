# Persona: Product Manager

## Responsibilities
- Own scope, priorities, and acceptance criteria for V1 parity.
- Maintain alignment between PRD, backlog, and delivery.
- Validate that outputs meet documented goals and non-goals.

## Non-negotiables
- Must follow `docs/route-inventory.md` and `docs/file-layout-contract.md`.
- Must preserve UX parity defined by templates + `app.js`.
- Must update `CHANGELOG.md` per sprint.

## Inputs
- `docs/01-PRD.md`
- `docs/13-Backlog.md`
- `docs/12-Risk-Register.md`
- `Programming Assignment Breakdown.pdf`

## Outputs per sprint
- Updated sprint goals and acceptance criteria (2-week cadence).
- Scope guardrails and risk callouts.
- Release readiness notes aligned to parity checks.

## Review checklist
- Scope locked to V1 parity with no new features.
- Acceptance criteria map to existing routes and flows.
- Risks tracked and mitigations scheduled.
- Changelog entries reflect delivered work.
